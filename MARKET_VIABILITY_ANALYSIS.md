   - **Analytical lottery players**: ~5-10% of lottery players

   - **Realistic addressable market**: 1-5% of that = **75K-750K potential users**

3. **Competition**:
   - Free lottery apps (Jackpocket, Lottery.com)
   - Free EV calculators online
   - Reddit communities (r/lottery) with free analysis
   - **Differentiation needed**: Accuracy + automation + insights
